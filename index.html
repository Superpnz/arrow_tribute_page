Brief description of the hero:

Oliver Queen, also known as Green Arrow, is a billionaire vigilante from DC Comics. After being stranded on a deserted island, he mastered archery and combat skills. Returning to Star City, he fights crime using his bow, high-tech arrows, and strong moral code. Unlike Batman, he’s more outspoken and liberal, defending the poor and oppressed. His iconic phrase: "You have failed this city!"
